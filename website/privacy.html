ChronoPanel Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

ChronoPanel is a local-only Chrome extension that estimates browser usage from Chrome history and stores summary data on your device.

Information Used

The extension may access Chrome history visit timestamps and URLs to estimate browsing time. By default, summaries are stored using domains. Full URL storage is optional and disabled by default.

Information Not Collected

ChronoPanel does not collect passwords, payment information, form entries, message content, or page content.

Data Storage

All extension data is stored locally using Chrome storage on your device. No cloud sync or external database is used.

Data Sharing

ChronoPanel does not sell, transfer, or send your browsing data to third parties.

Controls

You can clear all stored data from the Settings screen inside the extension. You can also export your local data as JSON.
